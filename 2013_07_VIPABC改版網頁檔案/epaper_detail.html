With the advent of social media and blogs, it’s now easier than ever to become famous, or to “brand” yourself. While you might not be the next Nike or Apple, how you come across on these platforms can be really important when it comes to being successful. So what is it you want to be known for? How should you brand yourself?

First , think about what it is that makes up your brand. Use positive words, such as collaborative, resourceful, connected, diplomatic, visionary, etc., when describing your persona or outlook. If you’re a tax accountant, brand yourself to sound like an expert in the field. While you’re at it, build an online presence. If people were to Google search your name, what results would they get? What they find should be separate from your company’s website.

Go on to network in order to generate brand awareness. You want to connect with other professionals in similar industries by using social networks and commenting on other people’s blogs. This is one of the best ways for you to become known in the industry. Form relationships with people, especially your audience!
