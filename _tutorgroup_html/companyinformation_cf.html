COMPANY FACTS

Through VIPABC.com and TutorABC.com—TutorGroup’s English-language learning platforms offered in mainland China and Taiwan, respectively—students have 24/7 access to more than 2,000 tutors, most of whom are TESOL certified, native English language speakers that live in the United States.


VIPABC.com and TutorABC.com have served more than 5 million classroom sessions, combining for more than 10,000 hours of course content.


The number of active students TutorGroup currently serves is in the high 5 digits range, which is comparable to the University of Georgia and University of California-Berkeley in terms of total enrollment among US universities.


TutorGroup has achieved rapid growth over the last 10 years through its proprietary online education platform, which dynamically recommends the optimal course size, the best teacher, and the right teaching material from more than 10,000 hours of existing course content.

Winners of the Red Herring Top 100 Global award as a top global company in 2012, TutorGroup consistently earns top satisfaction ratings from users for high-quality teachers, content and support. The average student learns on TutorGroup’s platform for three and a half years, with more than 95% of learners renewing their contracts month-over-month.


Further, TutorGroup forecasts the online English language-learning segment within China is growing at 25% annually, and will grow to more than U.S. $17 billion by 2015. Within China alone, TutorGroup’s sales expect to enjoy 3 digits annual growth rate in the years to come.


Busy professionals can take classes from their mobile devices at any time using the TutorMobile® classroom apps for iOS and Android.
