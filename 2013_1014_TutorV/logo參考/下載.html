The bold design work of Ragnar Freyr

Ragnar Freyr is a graphic designer and illustrator from ReykjavÃ­k, Iceland. I’d also describe him as a really talented guy. Can’t remember how I came across his work but I was immediately impressed by how purely graphic it is. There’s not wasted lines or flourishes, just clean, bold marks.

I feel like I don’t see enough of this kind of work lately, which could be described as being very Rand-esque. He also has a really nice blog called Createmake which he’s been running since 2007. It’s got lots of great video content on there, a lot of which was really new to me. Finally, he has an amazing little pug named Panda which you can see in this super cute video. All around Ragnar clearly has a really good life.
