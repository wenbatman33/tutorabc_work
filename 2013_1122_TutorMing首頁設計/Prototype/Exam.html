Is AP Chinese Exam only taken by students who are always get good grades?

AP Chinese Exam is for all students who plan to be academic prepared and motivated to take on college-level course. Your AP score can plus your qualification and make you stand out among all candidates.

How long will it take to achieve the level to get score 5?
