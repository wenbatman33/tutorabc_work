Awards and Accolades

 

Founded in 2004, TutorGroup created the first commercially available synchronous learning portal in the world and developed four prominent service product businesses including VIPABC.com, TutorABC.com, TutorABCJr.com for English training. To date TutorGroup has conducted over 5 million classes in 14 countries.

 

In 2012, TutorGroup was selected as a 2012 Red Herring Top 100 Global. The Red Herring 100 Global Award committee conducts a rigorous review of the achievements and long-term goals of thousands of enterprises and selects the 100 most innovative private companies in the hi-tech field. The idea behind this award is to propel these companies onto the world stage.

 

Later on, with the rising amount of people who want to learn Mandarin Chinese, TutorMing was launched in 2008 to satisfy learners worldwide. Nowadays, TutorMing.com is today the global pioneer in Mandarin Chinese solution e-learning via cloud computing technology. Now you can truly learn Chinese anytime and anywhere, live on the Internet with our Chinese language consultants. TutorMing offers an open and dynamic organization, user-friendly lesson management processes and a comprehensive study evaluation mechanism to resolve the problems with traditional study resources.
