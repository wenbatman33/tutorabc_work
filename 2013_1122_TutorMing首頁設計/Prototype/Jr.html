Learning vs. Studying 

Most children start attending Chinese school or begin learning with a Chinese tutor at a young age. Tuition is no small expense, but do you know how well your children are doing? Did Chinese school really help them?

 

Every child should be treated as an individual, with personalized learning methods, individualized topics, and customized learning plans, just like an adult. They need someone to understand their needs, their strengths, and their weaknesses. Learning and memorization are two different things. Let them learn Chinese the way they learned their first language and stop forcing them to learn Chinese with a method they don’t enjoy.

 

Provide the best opportunity

Don’t worry about forcing your children to study. Provide your children with the opportunity to enjoy a Harvard-style classroom experience while learning Chinese in a fun and easy way!

 

We also offer pre-exam lessons to build on learners’ vocabularies. Without them knowing, learners will be prepared for future examinations that can be used to apply for college and universities.

Regular classes for teens are designed to give young adults systematic training in all four aspects of Chinese language: listening, speaking, reading and writing. TutorMing certified Chinese consultants make learning Chinese fun and easy by discussing interesting topics and stories, the same way we all learned our first language.

 

For teens who have never taken Chinese classes before and cannot read Pinyin or Chinese characters, we will start with basic topics such as greetings, self-introductions, etc. Pinyin and basic strokes of Chinese characters will also be introduced. As learners advance, more complex material will be presented, and students will be able to give critical views regarding different topics using Chinese like a native speaker. Pre-exam classes are offered to build on students’ vocabularies and prepare them for future college entry exams.
