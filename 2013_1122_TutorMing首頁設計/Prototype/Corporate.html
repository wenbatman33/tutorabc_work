Corporate Clients

TutorMing Corporate Solutions provide a complete training package for any size corporation to optimize Chinese learning. Each training solution is customized based on a company's requirements whether at a department level or position level and based on a company's specific goals. TutorMing provides each company with a set of management and analysis tools to help monitor, manage and evaluate learning progress at an individual, department and corporate level. TutorMing Corporate Solutions is the next step for any corporation to become a dominant global player in the international market.

What Can We Provide for Corporate Clients:

Anytime Anywhere

No matter if you are at home or at the office, on a business trip, or traveling, TutorMing helps corporate clients study Chinese all around the world. TutorMing provides service at the most convenient times and loations which your employees are never limited to a specific location and don't waste time, money, and energy on transportation.

Comprehensive study follow-up system and progress report

TutorMing system keeps a detailed record of the conditions present during each session. This allows for tracking of each learner‘s progress and analysis of each learner's special situations so that learning difficulties can be overcome.

Core training benefits

To provide corporate members with employee data for analysis by its Human Resources department, the TutorMing system compiles statistics based on number of sessions attended, type of session focus, progress curve, and materials selection. This allows a corporate member to assess the efficacy and cost efficiency of its Chinese training program.

Customized study plan and learning materail for corporate clients

We customize the most appropriate study contents to fit each individual's needs. This dynamic course content is not the old-fashioned, static approach to learning.
