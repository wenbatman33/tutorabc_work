We believe that that education is undergoing a paradigm shift as students increasingly demand high quality, flexible, personalized learning through online education

 

Our Goal is Beyond Expectations

 

There are five core areas across which TutorGroup differentiates itself from both online and offline competition:

Personalized: The user experience is at the heart of everything we do.

 

TutorGroup’s patented Dynamic Course Generation System (DCGS): Our sophisticated, dynamic technology platform ensures that learners are matched with the teachers, materials and fellow students that best suit their needs.

 

Our system can track multiple variables for each learner – including learning requirements, preferred learning style, preferred teaching style, language needs, and strong/weak points.

 

Flexibility: Flexible and able to meet your busy schedule

 

Before work? After work? At work? Anytime.

Study Mandarin at home, and use it wherever you need it.

At home? At the office? On a business trip? Anywhere.

You are never limited to a specific location. Don't waste time, money, and energy on transportation.

 

Quality: Student-centered platform with world-class service. Rate us on our teachers, material, and IT support.

 

Our platform also gathers and learns from user feedback. Every teacher, every class and every piece of teaching material is rated by our users and ranked accordingly, putting the user perspective at the heart of our offering. This allows us to provide personalized, high-quality instruction to everyone.

 

Certified consultants worldwide: Strict teaching requirements and screening for consultants-

TutorMing has hundreds of specialized Chinese consultants worldwide. Every consultant has had rich teaching experience and specializes in his/her profession.

 

Small classes: a maximum of four students with similar language abilities and interests to enhance learning effectiveness.

 

Technology: 24/7 technical support center monitor service quality

 

TutorConsole and 24H Operation Center: The centralized monitoring and supporting system run by our 24x7 operation crew provides immediate support to every student and teacher.

 

TutorMeet and TutorMobile: patented Video/Voice over IP platform that creates a live learning environment online for various sizes of sessions.

 

CloudAgent: A logistic system designed for global management of the whole company’s human resources, especially home-based teachers. Using this technology, we recruit, train, assign sessions, and communicate with teachers around the world, all through Internet.

 

TutorGroup’s mobile platform provides the same convenient learning environment on the go! Check out available learning tools now.

 

Fun: Say goodbye to textbooks and learn only what you need

 

The DCGS selects topics that match your areas of interest. With TutorMing, you don’t have to learn chapters of a textbook that don’t apply to you, but just the topics you need for work, school, daily life, or tests.

 

We provide a fully interactive learning environment that combines the learning quality of a classroom education with the individual flexibility of online learning.

 

One-way webcam: Anonymity for all students. Enjoy privacy in an environment that will help you feel at ease practicing English and speaking with your consultant and classmates.

 

Our innovative approach has changed how people interact and collaborate online, enabling ubiquitous lifelong learning environments for all ages and professions across geographic areas.
